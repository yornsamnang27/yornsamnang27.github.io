2024 Cambodian University Ranking

What are the most popular Colleges and Universities in Cambodia? uniRank answers this question by publishing the 2024 Cambodian University Ranking of 49 Cambodian higher-education institutions meeting the following uniRank selection criteria:

  • being chartered, licensed or accredited by the appropriate Cambodian higher education-related organization
  • offering at least three-year bachelor's degrees or postgraduate master's or doctoral degrees
  • delivering courses predominantly in a traditional, non-distance education format
    • uniRank aims to provide a non-academic League Table of the top Cambodian Universities and Colleges based on valid, unbiased and non-influenceable web metrics provided by independent web intelligence sources rather than data submitted by the Universities themselves.
